Trade-In Policy

At Success Through Sweat, we understand that your fitness needs may change over time. That's why we offer a hassle-free trade-in policy for your used sports and exercise equipment. Whether you're upgrading to newer models or simply looking to switch up your workout routine, we're here to help. Simply bring in your gently-used equipment to any of our branches, and our knowledgeable staff will assess its condition and value. You can then use the credit towards the purchase of new equipment from our store. It's our way of ensuring that you can always stay on top of your fitness game without breaking the bank.
